About me

I am an Computer Scientist and Engineer with a Ph.D. in Information and Communication Technologies by the University of Trento in Italy.

Currently, I am a research and development postdoc at UC Berkeley and Inria, where I work with Prof. James Holston and Dr. Valerie Issarny in the AppCivist Project. I also collaborate remotely with the team of Participa at the Catholic University of Asunción.

Research Section

In my years of professional research and work, I have had experience working with a myriad of topics, including mainly the following: User experience and HCI research, qualitative research methods, participatory design, participatory action research, web engineering, service oriented computing (particularly RESTful based service platforms), data and knowledge management, mobile applications design and implementation.

More recently, I have been focusing my work in the multidisciplinary realm of Social-Community Informatics and Human Computer Interactions, using Participatory Design and Participatory Action Research as my principal approaches to the work in these fields.
